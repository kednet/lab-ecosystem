## 📚 Recap
=========

**Past Simple vs Present Perfect — главные маркеры:**

| Past Simple | Present Perfect |
|---|---|
| yesterday | ever / never |
| last week / month / year | just / already / yet |
| ago | since / for |
| in 2020 | recently / this week |
| when I was young | so far |

**Запомни:**
- Конкретное время → Past Simple (даже если "ago")
- Опыт в жизни → Present Perfect (ever, never, twice)
- Результат в настоящем → Present Perfect (I have finished = готово сейчас)
- Недавнее событие → Present Perfect (just, already, yet)
